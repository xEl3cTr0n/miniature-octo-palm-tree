# MIS-to-Technical Role Positioning

ClaimLens is especially suitable for an MIS major because it combines business process analysis with technical execution.

## Why it fits an MIS background

MIS coursework often emphasizes systems, databases, business workflows, controls, and decision support. ClaimLens uses those strengths in a more technical setting by turning an evidence-review workflow into a working AI system.

## Roles this can support

- AI business systems analyst moving toward applied AI engineering.
- Junior or associate AI engineer.
- Full-stack developer with AI product focus.
- Data engineer or analytics engineer interested in unstructured data.
- Technical product analyst for AI workflow tools.

## Skills to highlight on a resume

- Built a FastAPI service for citation-backed evidence question answering.
- Implemented retrieval, chunking, workflow checklists, and evaluation utilities in Python.
- Designed a multimodal AI architecture covering documents, images, audio, video, and structured metadata.
- Created a roadmap for production RAG, LLM evaluation, observability, and human-in-the-loop review.

## Recommended learning path while building

1. Python testing, typing, APIs, and packaging.
2. SQL, PostgreSQL, pgvector, and data modeling.
3. RAG fundamentals: chunking, embeddings, reranking, citations, and evals.
4. Multimodal AI: OCR, ASR, image embeddings, and document models.
5. Deployment: Docker, CI, environment variables, monitoring, and cloud hosting.
